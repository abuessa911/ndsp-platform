# DSP NMP v1 Policy

NMP is a price zone, not an indicator value.

Operational version:
- Momentum detector: RSI(7)
- Output shown to user: NMP Price Zone
- Bullish direction: Open price of the last opposite bearish candle before the highest bullish momentum candle.
- Bearish direction: Open price of the last opposite bullish candle before the strongest bearish momentum candle.
- Internal logic, formulas, weights, and raw calculations remain hidden.

Visible label:
- NMP Price Zone
- Arabic: منطقة NMP السعرية
