# NDSP Legal Trial Entry Acknowledgment Policy

The platform must show legal/trial acknowledgment before entry unless accepted in browser.

Required contents:
1. Legal disclaimer.
2. Owner liability protection.
3. Statement that NDSP is decision support and contextual analysis.
4. Statement that NDSP is not an investment firm.
5. Statement that NDSP does not provide binding financial recommendations.
6. Statement that NDSP does not manage funds.
7. Statement that NDSP does not guarantee results or profits.
8. 16-day trial notice.
9. Trial purpose.
10. End-of-trial feedback/survey requirement.
11. Registered user offer or activation priority statement.
12. Explicit acceptance checkbox/button.
13. Arabic and English language switch.
