# NDSP Public Platform Bot Privacy Boundary

Generated: 20260629_093055

## Final Rule

The public NDSP decision-support platform must not mention, promote, link to, or expose the execution bot.

## Public Platform Scope

NDSP public/user-facing platform is limited to:

- Decision support
- Market reading
- Scenario reference levels
- Risk radar
- Decision quality
- Governance language
- Educational and institutional user experience

## Forbidden on Public Platform

The following must not appear in public NDSP pages:

- Bot pages
- Bot navigation links
- Execution cockpit
- Execution-layer wording
- Telegram private execution service references
- Any implication that the public platform executes trades

## Private Bot Scope

The bot is private, personal, and not announced inside NDSP public platform.

The bot may be offered separately through private Telegram workflow only, outside the public decision-support user journey.

## Product Boundary

NDSP = decision support.

Private Telegram Bot = separate execution channel.

These two must remain commercially and visually separated.
